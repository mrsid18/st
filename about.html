ABOUT  US.

Our mission is to develop the western consumerism drive which is gradually gaining momentum. And to develop international flavours and create an Indo-Western consumerism cohort which lets every Indian enjoy international flavours and snacks.

At Snack Terminal we strive to make quality products and mind-blowing flavours to create a global Indo-Western brand which puts India in the forefront of the snacking industry. Thereby, creating an ecosystem of Indianized taste.

India has been evolving its snacking habits ever since independence. Our diverse culture has given us various snack legends - products that have gone on to capture even the international markets. However, with the rise of the western consumers; Indo-Western culture has been growing. On the contrary, our snacking habits and the consequent food options available to us haven't been diverse enough to capture the true western consumerism. This inevitably brings us to question the availability of diverse options, Westernised Snacks and International flavours in the Indian Subcontinent.
